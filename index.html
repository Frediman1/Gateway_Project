Welcome to Sierra Leone


Sierra leone is a country in west africa that was founded by a Portuguese sailor known as Pedro Da Cintra in 1462. It then became a colony of The Great Britian and later gained Independece about 59 years ago. Sierra Leone is known for it's riches in natural resources such as Gold and Diamond, but also for it's beautiful beaches and islands. It's also has beautiful natural wildlife conservation and is home to millions of species. The night life in the capital city of Freetown will leave you with butterfly feelings and will have you wanting for more.
